About

Get off the beaten path with this hiking tour, led by a local guide. You'll head up into the woods, taking in views of the surrounding hills, adorned with prayer flags, while you breathe in fresh mountain air. The hike is suitable for both beginners and more experienced hikers and the total distance is roughly 4-5 kilometers (2.5-3 miles) round-trip.
